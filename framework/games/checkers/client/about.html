Checkers

A classic 1v1 board game where players move diagonally and capture opponent pieces by jumping.

Rules

  • Players take turns moving one piece diagonally forward by one square.
  • If an opponent piece is adjacent and the square behind it is free, you must capture by jumping.
  • Multiple captures in one turn are allowed when available.
  • Reaching the opposite end promotes a piece to a king, which can move diagonally both forward and backward.
  • You win by capturing all opponent pieces or leaving the opponent without legal moves.
