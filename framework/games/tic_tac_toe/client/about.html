Tic Tac Toe

A quick 2-player game played on a 3x3 grid.

Rules

  • Players alternate turns placing their mark (X or O) into an empty square.
  • The first player to align three marks in a row, column, or diagonal wins.
  • If all squares are filled with no three-in-a-row, the game is a draw.
  • Only one mark can occupy a square, and moves cannot be undone.
